Come, Thou Fount of Every Blessing
  1. Come, Thou Fount of every blessing,
  2. Tune my heart to sing Thy grace;
  3. Streams of mercy, never ceasing,
  4. Call for songs of loudest praise.
  5. Jesus sought me when a stranger,
  6. Wand’ring from the face of God;
  7. He, to save my soul from danger,
  8. Interposed His precious blood.
  1. O to grace how great a debtor
  2. Daily I’m constrained to be!
  3. Let that grace, Lord, like a fetter,
  4. Bind my wand’ring heart to Thee.
  5. Teach me, Lord, some rapturous measure,
  6. Meet for me Thy grace to prove,
  7. While I sing the countless treasure
  8. Of my God’s unchanging love.
  1. Prone to wander, Lord, I feel it;
  2. Prone to leave the God I love:
  3. Take my heart, oh, take and seal it
  4. With Thy Spirit from above.
  5. Rescued thus from sin and danger,
  6. Purchased by the Savior’s blood,
  7. May I walk on earth a stranger,
  8. As a son and heir of God.
Come, Thou Fount of Every Blessing
